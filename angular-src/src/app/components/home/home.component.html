What is Craftmelon all about?


The crafts of India are diverse, rich in history and religion. The craft of each state in India reflect the influence of different empires. Throughout centuries, crafts have been embedded as a culture and tradition within Indians.


Craftmelon offers a unique blend of contemporary, urbane, stylish, furniture with a celebration of Indian flavour. Craftmelon furniture designs are not only functional but also exemplify a deep aesthetic sense that will add character to every home. Every furniture is personally handcrafted by trained craftsman and artisans who ensure the perfect finish and quality that is worthy for household.


The traditions of Indian arts and crafts are rooted deeply in the lap of history. There are more than 600 plus art form in India, Craftmelon wants to fuse these art form to the contemporary design and give a new modern look to these art forms. Our collection of exclusive designs are personally designed by in house designers to ensure the furniture uses the art form which fits every house hold.


No doubt its a reality that our age-old traditions are dying in this world of fast-paced development. Artisans pertaining to these art and craft forms are also in parallel facing an uphill task to retain them. Much less ubiquitous is the understanding, the backing and the encouragement required to ensure that such magic is not lost to future generations through neglect and apathy. This is a real concern as the march towards modernity continues. It is the spur for initiatives such as those pursued with fervor by CraftMelon.

What is Craftmelon Strategy?

India has more than 1000 different type of art form and Handicrafts and many in these are almost dying. Craftmelon wants to use these art form and wanted to give modern look to it, So that it could be used in modern homes. We wanted fuse these art form to the contemporary furniture design.


By doing this we are supporting the artisans for their live hood and also trying to retrieve the lost art form. These artisans will be having work only during the festivals seasons, as these arts are gifted only during festivals. We at Craftmelon wanted to employee these artsian so that they have work year long and also there job safety.


Craftmelon encourages youngster who are interested to pursue their career in art furniture to learn them and we also wanted to ensure that they have a job with us to form their career.


How Craftmelon on blockchain work?

Craftmelon will have a unique ID issued for all the products and art work.


These details will be updated in the blockchain network . On doing this we will be able to track the product in blockchain network. This would also help in knowing the genuinity of the product.
